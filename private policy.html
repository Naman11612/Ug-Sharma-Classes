Privacy Policy

Welcome to UG Sharma Classes. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our educational services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you register for our courses or services, we may collect personal information such as:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Educational background
  • Payment information (processed securely by our payment partners)

Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you interact with our website, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Downloaded materials (notes, PPTs, etc.)
  • Search queries

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our educational services
  • To process enrollments and payments
  • To deliver study materials (notes, PPTs, etc.)
  • To improve our website and services
  • To communicate with you about courses, updates, and promotions
  • To monitor and analyze usage patterns
  • To prevent fraud and enhance security

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Secure servers with encryption
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal data
  • Secure payment processing

However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Study Materials Access

When you download study materials (notes, PPTs, etc.) from our website:

  • We track which materials you access for analytics and improvement purposes
  • Materials are for personal educational use only
  • Redistribution or commercial use of materials is prohibited

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Personalize content
  • Track downloads of study materials

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect website functionality.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as:

  • Payment processors
  • Analytics providers
  • Cloud storage for study materials
  • Email service providers

These third parties have access to your information only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for other purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for students aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will take steps to delete the information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
